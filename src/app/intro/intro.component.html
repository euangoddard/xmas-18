The challenge

Father Christmas needs your help to find his lost presents in the snow.

Move Father Christmas up, down, left or right one square at a time to find out what's under the snow.

The aim of the game is to collect all the presents in the least number of moves – but watch out, if you stumble across the Grinch , the level will be failed.
