The challenge

The aim of the game is to help Father Christmas find his lost presents in the snow.

You start each level with a snow covered board and Father Christmas visible, He can move up, down, left or right one sqaure at a time.

The aim of the game is to collect all the presents in the least number of moves – but watch out, if you stumble across the Grinch , the level will be failed.
